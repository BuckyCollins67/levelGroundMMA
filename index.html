WHERE GRIT MEETS OPPORTUNITY

At Level Ground (LG), urban youth cultivate grit and resilience through Mixed Martial Arts. They learn to apply these values ‘off the mat’ in the pursuit of their ambitions through our Student Trainer Certification and College Access programs.

WHY

At Level Ground, we believe in harnessing the discipline, resilience, and tenacity inherent in Mixed Martial Arts to empower our youth. Our mission is not only to cultivate elite fighters but to forge strong-minded individuals. Through rigorous training, mentorship, and community support, we aim to provide a sanctuary where young minds can grow, challenge themselves, and unlock their fullest potential. Beyond the mat, we strive to instill values of respect, responsibility, and determination, ensuring that our students are equipped to face life's battles with confidence.
